# 🚆 Tara Tren

![Tara Tren Banner](assets/image/Gemini_Generated_Image_wfqe9iwfqe9iwfqe.png)

> **Your Unified Transit Companion for Metro Manila.**

### 📥 [Download Latest Release (v0.3.0)](https://github.com/KyahFranzz10/TaraTren/releases)

**TaraTren** is a modern, community-driven mobile application designed to simplify the commuting experience across the Philippines' capital. It consolidates all major rail lines, provides real-time crowd insights, and visualizes the future of Manila's transit expansion.

---

## 🌟 Key Features

### 🛤️ Unified Rail Dashboard
*   **LRT-1, LRT-2, MRT-3, and PNR (Soon!):** Real-time visibility of all operating lines in one place.
*   **Station Details:** Access accessibility information (Elevators, Escalators), nearby landmarks, connecting routes and platform side directions (Door opening side).
*   **Rolling Stock Integration:** View official train generation and technical details for the fleet.

### 👥 Community-Driven Insights
*   **Live Crowd Density:** Know if a station is "Light," "Moderate," or "Heavy" before you even arrive.
*   **Social Pulse:** Report and view live community status updates directly from fellow commuters.
*   **Peak Hour Intelligence:** Data-driven charts suggesting the best times to travel.

### 💰 Savings & Advocacy
*   **Cost Comparison:** Compare taking the train vs. driving (including fuel and parking).
*   **Environmental Impact:** See your contribution to CO2 reduction by choosing public transit.
*   **Digital Trip Journal:** Keep track of your commutes and overall spending.

### 🛡️ Security & Identity
*   **Database Hardening:** Granular Row Level Security (RLS) ensuring your trip journal and profile are 100% private.
*   **Commuter Identity Hub:** Centralized profile management with high-fidelity social avatar support.

### 🗺️ Future Manila Rail Network
*   Explore the road ahead with interactive maps of the **MRT-7**, **Metro Manila Subway (MMS)**, and **North-South Commuter Railway (NSCR)**.

### 🔊 Accessibility & Convenience
*   **Dynamic Island Transit Tracker:** Real-time, system-wide floating overlay (LRT/MRT) inspired by iOS, providing speed, distance-to-next-station, and arrival notifications.
*   **Visual Mirror Perspective:** The Dynamic Island carousel automatically flips to match your perspective (Left/Right side) based on the upcoming station's door side.
*   **Custom Voice Pack Announcements:** Bilingual (Tagalog/English) announcements with various personalities (Professional, Casual, Conyo).
*   **Walking Transfer Guides:** Step-by-step visual paths for seamless line interchanges.
*   **Smart Advisory Alerts:** Intelligent scraping of real-time technical issues and service disruptions with native device push notifications.
*   **Speedometer:** Monitor live train speed and pace during your journey.

## 🛠️ Technical Stack

-   **Frontend:** [Flutter](https://flutter.dev/) (3.0+)
-   **Backend:** [Supabase](https://supabase.com/) & [Firebase](https://firebase.google.com/)
-   **Mapping:** [flutter_map](https://pub.dev/packages/flutter_map) with OpenStreetMap, CartoDB, and ESRI World Imagery.
-   **Offline Caching:** Custom SQL-based storage for map tiles and alerts.

## 📲 Getting Started
TaraTren is currently available for Android devices via direct APK installation.

### Platform Support
*   **Android**: Available now via the Releases page.
*   **iOS / Web**: Available via [TaraTren Web](https://KyahFranzz10.github.io/TaraTren). 
    *   *Tip: On iOS, open in Safari and tap "Add to Home Screen" for a full-screen experience.*

### How to Install (Android)
1.  **Download the APK**: Go to the [Official Releases](https://github.com/KyahFranzz10/TaraTren/releases) page.
2.  **Choose your Architecture**: Download the APK that matches your device (usually `v8a` for modern phones).
3.  **Enable Unknown Sources**: If prompted, allow your browser or file manager to install apps from unknown sources.
4.  **Install & Launch**: Open the downloaded `.apk` file and follow the on-screen prompts.
5.  **Safe Travels!**: Open TaraTren and start tracking your commute.
